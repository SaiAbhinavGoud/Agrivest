Artificial Intelligence can radically change the course of how farmers utilise their lands. Using predictive machine learning models like yield prediction, crop recommendation system and humidity forecasting, farmers are able to make informed and efficient decisions about their cultivation. This reduces the costs that they bear and creates a better environmental impact.

Yield Prediction – Factors used – District, Crop, Season Year, Previous Season Yield to predict the yield if a crop is planted in an area.

Crop Recommendations – Factors used – Soil composition, Rainfall, Humidity, Temperature, pH – to give the best crop that a farmer might grow on his land.

Humidity Predictor – Factors Used – Area, Minimum and Maximum Humidity, Minimum and Maximum Temperature – Used to optimize irrigation schedules, adjust planting dates, and take other measures to protect crops from mould and disease.
